1. Introduction

SlimBuddy (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is a virtual assistant designed to help users track meals, Syn values, weight, and progress based on Slimming World–style plans. We are not affiliated with Slimming World and do not provide official advice.

2. Data We Collect

  • Profile Info: Name, preferences.
  • Health Data: Meals, Syns, weight, exercise logs.
  • Auth Data: Email and secure token.

3. How We Use It

  • To personalize your SlimBuddy experience.
  • We do NOT sell your data.

4. Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (GDPR-compliant). Tokens are encrypted and HTTPS is enforced.
