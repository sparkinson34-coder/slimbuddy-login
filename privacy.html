SlimBuddy Privacy Policy

Last Updated:1st August 2025

1. Introduction

SlimBuddy (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is a virtual assistant designed to help users track meals, Syn values, weight, and progress based on Slimming World–style plans. We are not affiliated with Slimming World and do not provide official advice.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Profile Information: Name, preferences (tone, diet type, weight units).
  • Health Data: Meal logs, Syn values, weight entries, exercise logs.
  • Authentication Data: Login email and token for secure access.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To log and display your meals, weight, and goals.
  • To personalize your SlimBuddy experience.
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, a GDPR-compliant database service. Login credentials and tokens are encrypted and used only for session validation. We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers.

5. How to Delete Your Data

You can request account deletion and full data removal by emailing: support@yourslimbuddy.com.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – data storage and authentication.
  • Railway – backend hosting.
  • Netlify – login page hosting.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
